**1. Local Sale**


<img width="678" height="405" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4455b728-b877-4f1c-8249-ce42f71a8b08" />


**Local Sale Page** helps users monitor overall **local sales performance** by comparing actual results with _budget_ and _year-ago_ figures, while also tracking trends over time to understand growth patterns. It provides a detailed breakdown of how much each country contributes to total sales, highlights changes and performance differences across brands, and identifies top-performing as well as underperforming products, enabling users to clearly see where growth is coming from and where improvements are needed.

**2. Shipment**


<img width="677" height="402" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f1575630-9578-4bcb-a6c6-ef21b5f8b4c8" />


**Shipment Page ** helps users monitor overall shipment performance by comparing **actual shipments** with _budget_ and _year-ago_ figures, while also tracking progress against targets to evaluate execution efficiency. It provides visibility into shipment trends over time, highlights how much each country contributes to total shipments, and shows performance gaps across months.

In addition, it breaks down shipment results by brand and product, capturing both absolute changes and growth rates to identify which brands are driving improvements or facing declines. By combining contribution share, variance vs. budget, and year-on-year changes across countries, brands, and products, the dashboard enables users to pinpoint key drivers, spot underperformance, and support more accurate planning and operational decisions.

**3. Stock Projection**


<img width="678" height="403" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/883c7389-e240-44fa-9d3b-904dff7a8731" />


**Stock Projection Page** helps users monitor projected **stock coverage** over time by showing how many weeks of inventory are available across different countries and SKUs based on current orders. It highlights stock levels month by month, allowing users to quickly identify potential risks such as low coverage (risk of stockout) or unusually high coverage (overstock).

In addition, it provides a detailed breakdown of stock movement drivers such as loaded volume, sales, and stock on hand—at the product level, helping users understand what is causing changes in coverage. By combining country-level visibility with SKU-level detail, the dashboard enables better inventory planning, early risk detection, and more informed supply chain decisions.

**4. KPIs**


<img width="657" height="404" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/719c7208-39f7-4e06-abb3-07be7e21364c" />


**KPIs Page** helps users monitor key supply chain KPIs by evaluating **performance** against targets over time and across countries. It shows how well shipments are executed versus budget, tracks forecast accuracy and bias, and measures service levels and planning efficiency, with a clear view of monthly trends.

In addition, it compares KPI performance across different countries, highlighting variations in execution quality, forecasting accuracy, and operational consistency. By combining time-based and geographic views with target benchmarks, the dashboard enables users to identify gaps, monitor stability, and take corrective actions to improve overall supply chain performance.
